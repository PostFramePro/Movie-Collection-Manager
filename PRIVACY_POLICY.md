# Privacy Policy for Movie Collection Manager

**Effective Date:** January 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 4, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Movie Collection Manager ("the Application") is a desktop software application that helps users manage their personal movie collections and facilitates listing items for sale on eBay. This Privacy Policy explains how the Application collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the eBay integration features.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Local Data Storage
The Application stores the following information locally on your device:
- Movie collection data (titles, descriptions, images, purchase information)
- Item numbers and inventory tracking information
- Sale prices and transaction history
- User preferences and application settings

### 2.2 eBay Integration Data
When you connect your eBay account to the Application, we access and store:
- eBay OAuth authentication tokens (access token and refresh token)
- eBay user account information necessary for API communication
- Listing data and status information for items you create through the Application
- eBay selling activity and transaction data related to your listings

### 2.3 Third-Party API Data
The Application may retrieve supplemental movie information from:
- The Movie Database (TMDb) API for movie metadata
- UPC/barcode lookup services for product identification
- Image hosting services for product photography

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Uses
The Application uses your information to:
- Manage and organize your movie collection database
- Create, update, and manage eBay listings on your behalf
- Synchronize listing status between the Application and eBay
- Generate sales reports and inventory tracking
- Automatically populate listing details using movie database information

### 3.2 eBay-Specific Permissions
By authorizing the Application to access your eBay account, you grant permission to:
- View your eBay user account information
- Create new item listings in your eBay store
- Edit and revise existing listings created by the Application
- End or remove listings as directed by you
- View your eBay selling activity and marketing campaigns
- Access public data from eBay's marketplace

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage Only
- **All data is stored locally** on your personal computer
- The Application does NOT transmit your data to any remote servers (except as required for eBay API communication)
- Your movie collection database is stored in a local SQLite database file
- eBay authentication tokens are stored in encrypted local JSON files

### 4.2 eBay API Communication
- Communication with eBay's API uses secure HTTPS connections
- OAuth tokens are transmitted securely using industry-standard encryption
- The Application follows eBay's API security guidelines and best practices

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Your data remains on your device for as long as you use the Application
- You can delete your data at any time by removing the Application and its data files
- eBay tokens expire automatically and are refreshed as needed
- You can revoke eBay access at any time through your eBay account settings

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 eBay Platform
The Application integrates with eBay's platform using their official API. Your use of eBay services through this Application is also governed by:
- [eBay User Agreement](https://www.ebay.com/help/policies/member-behaviour-policies/user-agreement)
- [eBay Privacy Policy](https://www.ebay.com/help/policies/member-behaviour-policies/user-privacy-notice-privacy-policy)

### 5.2 Other Services
- **TMDb API**: Used for retrieving movie information. See [TMDb Privacy Policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy)
- **Image Hosting**: Any image hosting services used are subject to their own privacy policies

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 6.1 No Data Selling or Sharing
- We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
- We do NOT use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
- We do NOT collect analytics or usage statistics

### 6.2 eBay Data Transmission
The only data transmission that occurs is:
- Sending listing information to eBay when you create or update listings
- Receiving listing status and sales data from eBay to sync with your local database
- These transmissions occur only when you explicitly perform actions that require eBay communication

### 6.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information only if required by law, such as:
- In response to valid legal process (subpoena, court order)
- To protect our rights or property
- In emergency situations involving personal safety

## 7. Your Rights and Controls

### 7.1 Data Access and Control
You have complete control over your data:
- All data files are accessible on your local device
- You can export, backup, or delete your data at any time
- You can modify or delete any information stored in the Application

### 7.2 eBay Authorization Management
You can manage eBay access:
- Revoke the Application's access to your eBay account at any time through [eBay's Third-Party App Settings](https://signin.ebay.com/ws/eBayISAPI.dll?ThirdPartyAuthList)
- Disconnect your eBay account within the Application settings
- Tokens will expire automatically if not refreshed

### 7.3 Data Deletion
To completely remove your data:
- Uninstall the Application from your computer
- Delete the Application's data folder containing the database and settings files
- Revoke eBay authorization through eBay's account settings

## 8. Children's Privacy

This Application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. eBay's terms of service also require users to be at least 18 years old.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 10. International Users

This Application is designed for personal use and stores all data locally on your device. If you use eBay services in countries outside the United States, you are subject to eBay's international data transfer policies as outlined in their privacy policy.

This Application complies with eBay's Developer Program policies:
- We follow eBay's API Terms of Use

- We respect eBay's rate limits and usage guidelines
- We do not use eBay data for unauthorized purposes

By using the eBay integration features of Movie Collection Manager and authorizing the Application to access your eBay account, you consent to the collection, use, and storage of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
